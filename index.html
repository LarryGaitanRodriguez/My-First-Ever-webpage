His personal life timeline:

  • 1961 - Obama is born in Honolulu, Hawaii
  • 1979 - Graduated from Punahou Academy with honors
  • 1982 - Obama's father passes away from car accident.
  • 1983 - Graduated from Colombia Univesity with a degree in political science
  • 1985 - Moves to chicago
  • 1988 - Enters Harvard law school
  • 1992 - Marries Michelle Robinson; moves back to chicago
  • 1996 - Wins seat for Illinois state senate
  • 2004 - Wins seat for the US Senate
  • 2008 - Wins presidential election
  • 2012 - Wins second term as president
  • 2017 - His term ends and his transfers power to his successor, Donald Trump
  • 2017 - Resides with his family in Washington, DC
